The Hederodotiformes are a fascinating order of sharks, commonly known as carpet sharks. These sharks are recognized for their unique appearance, with broad, flat bodies and long, flexible pectoral fins. These sharks are typically found in tropical and subtropical waters, where they use their incredible camouflage to blend into the ocean floor.
